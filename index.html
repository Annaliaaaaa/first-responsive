Homestuck Beta Kids
John Eggbert:
John Eggbert is the main protagonist of the webcomic Homestuck. He is portrayed as your typical mischief making teen. He is shown to be incredibly fond of bad movies and to have an irrational hatred of the Betty Crocker brand.
Rose Lalonde:
Rose Lande is shown to be an intellectual through her obsession of lovecraftian literature and her planning the group’s movement throughout the story. She also enjoys knitting and speaks in a formal tone.
Dave Strider:
Dave Strider is the “cool guy” who only likes things ironically. He copies this behavior from his older brother. Dave is shown to be “smart-cynical”. He is John's best friend.
Jade Harley:
Jade is shown to be the most recalcitrant of the four. Jade can see into the future with her dreams and is therefore responsible for setting the plot into motion, recommending the other kids play Sburb.
